    Our Journey

    Best Staffing Solution


    Founded in December 2010, Durgamba Enterprises began with a dedicated team of 21 employees, leveraging our extensive experience and connections within the human resources departments of major job providers in our region. Our commitment to excellence and a robust network has enabled us to place over 2000 employees in diverse roles across various companies. Specialising in staffing and manpower solutions, we cater to a wide range of positions, including executives, managers, computer operators, and factory workers. Our strong relationships with law enforcement and worker unions support both labourers and factories, ensuring a seamless workflow for our clients.

    To further enhance our services, we established Soundarya Enterprises as a sister concern, allowing us to provide even better service and continue our growth driven by word-of-mouth and goodwill marketing. Our quality assurance team excels in payment processing and ESI PF processing, ensuring placements for housekeeping staff, line supervisors, managers, HR managers, skilled labourers, forklift operators, drivers, unskilled labourers, and security personnel. We strive to be a bridge between companies and employees, addressing issues directly with HR departments to ensure a smooth and efficient process for all involved.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to provide top-notch staffing and manpower solutions, bridging the gap between companies and employees through dedicated service, strong networks, and a commitment to quality. We aim to ensure smooth workflows and efficient processes for our clients by addressing and resolving issues promptly and effectively.



    Our Vision

    Our vision is to become the leading staffing solution provider in our region, renowned for our quality service, reliability, and strong relationships with both clients and employees. We aspire to continually grow and adapt, enhancing our services to meet the evolving needs of the job market and ensuring the success and satisfaction of all parties involved.
